Suprised Eye

Edited Image

Explanation

The reason why I chose this image was mainly because of its aesthetic and how memorable this day was for me. I took this photo during the time that the Canadian wildfires were happening and it stained the skies with a yellow and orange hue due to the resulting ash and smoke caused by the fires. This day was very memorable as I remember going out with my friends at the time and just walking around talking about the most random stuff. I remember the vibe being very weird as we constantly switched from talking about dumb subjects to the most deep and philosophical things. In addition to the skies being a yellow hue we could neither tell whether it was day or night.

For how I edited this image I tried to capture that same feeling by depicting my friends and I just hanging around. I used the draw tool to draw silhouettes of me and my friends. I choose a complementary color that contrasts the environment allowing us to stand out from everything else. I also added a drop shadow set to overlay on the silhouettes giving us that depth. In addition to this, I also added some shadows that were gaussian blurred making the drawn in subjects fit more into the environment rather than just slapped onto the image.
